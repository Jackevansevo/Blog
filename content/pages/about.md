This is my corner of the internet where I write about a variety of technical and
personal topics.

of freedom and autonomy whilst getting to grapple with interesting problems.

developments in the world of software development, programming and Linux.

My content isn't intended for a particular audience and is mostly an outlet for
self reflection. But if you do enjoy my content and would like to be notified
whenever I post you can subscribe via [RSS](/index.xml).
